Hello, I am David, a passionate Software Engineer who loves programming, learning new things and personal development in general. When it comes to IT, my main interests are 
**programming languaguages** - their design, implementation and analysis. I am interested and excited about the whole field ranging from language design, syntax and semantic analysis, type systems, static analysis, interpreters, compilers, debuggers and IDEs. 

Apart from that, I am also fond of **competitive programming**. You can sometimes find me competing on [Codeforces](https://codeforces.com/profile/dkozak94) though I can't really claim to be any good at it yet. I do it for fun. I also try to solve some tasks at [LeetCode](https://leetcode.com/dkozak94/) every now and then.

---

I live and work from Brno, a town in the beautiful land of Moravia in the Czech Republic. This year, I graduated from the [Faculty of Information Technology](https://www.fit.vut.cz/) at the [Brno University of Technology](https://www.vutbr.cz/). Currently, I work as a **Research Assistant** (read intern) in the [GraalVM team](https://www.graalvm.org/) at [Oracle Labs](https://labs.oracle.com/).
